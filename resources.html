How to Learn More about Chess

If you are interested in learning more about chess and improving your game you're in luck. There are tons of resources to help chess players of all skill levels improve. There are hundreds of websites, books, puzzles, and videos dedicated to chess. Here is a summary of some of the resources I use as a beginner.

Resources

Chess.com

  • ♟ Offers monthly or yearly subscriptions.

  • ♟ Subscription comes with access to thousands of puzzles, lessons, and videos written by experts.

  • ♟ Allows you to play and analyze chess agaisnt real opponents (or bots) online in many different game modes.

Daniel Naroditsky (Youtube Channel)

  • ♟ Free!

  • ♟ Hundreds of videos by Grandmaster Daniel Naroditsky featuring games, strategy, and openings.

  • ♟ Best for players towards the intermediate level of play.

Bobby Fischer Teaches Chess

  • ♟ $7.99 on Amazon.

  • ♟ One of the best selling chess books ever written by Grandmaster Bobby Fischer.

  • ♟ Highlights tactics from a beginner to advanced level.
